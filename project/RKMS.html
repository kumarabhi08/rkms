About School

Ramkrishna Mission School (ICSE) is an English medium school affiliated to the Indian Council of Secondary Education,Sidhgora,Jamshedpur.Classes begin all the way from Playgroup and go to XII standard. We offer the choice of Science subjects in Class XI and XII.Founded on 2nd October, 1969, the school is being run under the excellent guidance and management of Ramakrishna Mission Ashrama, Gwalior and is affiliated to Ramkrishna Math and Ramkrishna Mission, Belur Math, Kolkata West Bengal.The School has developed a unique blend of ancient and modern education.As we look to the future and embrace technological advances, we are guided by the teachings of Bhagwan Sri Ramakrishna,Maa Sharada and Swami Vivekananda which form the pillars of the education and values that the school strives to provide.These teachings inspire our students just as they have always inspired generations of people for the benefit of the nation.At the school,Man Making And Character Building, as envisaged by Swami Vivekananda is our main thrust area.The School strives to provide ample opportunity for all round development on not just academic but mental, physical, intellectual spiritual and social planes.Most of the school alumni have a proven track record and have successfully made their mark in this competitive world.The school is run under the able guidance of Swami Suprardiptananda Ji Maharaj who is the Principal of this school.A co-educational English medium school, RKM is spread over 7 acres of land area with huge playground right in the heart of the city.This playground is equipped for football and cricket, along with having a cemented basketball court and open space for morning assemblies.The School also has an Open Auditorium, space for Yoga and Meditation, Library hall, A.V. Rooms for online Tata ClassEdge Study, and separate laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology with latest gadgets and equipments.Recently the school has been enlisted for establishing Atal Tinkering Laboratory by Niti Aayog under Atal Innovation Mission,Government of India.
